Why Angular Table?

Angular Table is the first grid directive to support 100% declarative configuration, so you can use the full power of Angular.

Alternatives

Other grid directives require embedding huge strings of HTML and Angular templating inside javascript strings.

Extensibility

Simply use Angular templating to communicate with your controller. It just works as you'd expect.
